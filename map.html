Restorative Justice & Facilitation Theory via Research Integration Semester:

In order to manifest and persevere in creating continuity within the Primary Practicum Projects you presented last semester, we will be using several of in-class exercises in transformative justice, restorative justice and facilitation theory to create different avenues for outreach, community care and compassion.

Examples included: Mock Scenarios, Designing Outreach and Circle Drafting, Informational Podcast & Script Writing, and Digital Facilitation and Supervision.

This will help further develop systems to address and transform harmful conditions within your communities, to refine your initial project idea, revise using cumulative research integration and then you will complete an expanded showcase of the PPP via presentations, class and cohort check-ins, instructors shadowing meetings and/or digital facilitation by the end of the semester.
